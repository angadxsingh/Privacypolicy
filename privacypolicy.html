Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 May 2025

SkyRoute is a mobile drone delivery game developed as a student project. This privacy policy outlines how we handle user data and protect your privacy while using the game.

1. Data Collection

SkyRoute does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. We do not request login information, location, contacts, or any other identifiable data.

2. Device Permissions

The game may access basic device features like audio and screen orientation for gameplay purposes only. These are not used to collect or transmit any data.

3. Third-party Services

SkyRoute does not integrate third-party SDKs such as analytics or ads at this time. If added in future updates, the policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Data Storage

Game progress and user settings (like control preferences and volume levels) are stored locally on your device using Unity’s PlayerPrefs system. This information is not shared or accessible externally.

5. Children’s Privacy

SkyRoute is designed for general audiences. It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 and is compliant with child safety standards.

6. Updates to Policy

Any future updates that change how we handle user data will be reflected on this page. Users will be notified through the Play Store description or update logs.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: angadnahal@gmail.com

Thank you for playing SkyRoute!
